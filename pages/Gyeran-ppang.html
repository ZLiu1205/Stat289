Gyeran-ppang

Gyeran-ppang ( 계란빵 ; "egg bread") is a warm street snack sold throughout South Korea . [1] The fluffy, oblong-shaped loaf of bread is sweet and savory with a whole egg inside the pancake dough. [2] [3]
