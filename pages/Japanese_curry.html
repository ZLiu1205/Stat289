Japanese curry

Japanese curry ( カレー , karē ) is one of the most popular dishes in Japan. [1] It is commonly served in three main forms: curry rice ( カレーライス , karē raisu , curry over rice) , curry udon (curry over noodles ), and curry bread (a curry-filled pastry). Curry rice is most commonly referred to simply as "curry" ( カレー , karē ) .
