Galbi-jjim

Galbi-jjim [1] ( 갈비찜 ) or braised short ribs [1] is a variety of jjim or Korean steamed dish made with galbi (갈비, short rib). Beef galbi is sometimes referred to as "gari" (가리), so the dish can be called "garijjim" . [2] Galbijjim is generally made with beef or pork short ribs. In the latter case, it is called dweji galbijjim (돼지갈비찜). [3]
