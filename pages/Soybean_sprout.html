Soybean sprout

Soybean sprout is a culinary vegetable grown by sprouting soybeans . It can be grown by placing and watering the sprouted soybeans in the shade until the roots grow long. Soybean sprouts are extensively cultivated and consumed in Korea .

Kongnamul ( hangul : 콩나물 ) literally means soybean sprouts in the Korean language , but often refers to the seasoned banchan (sidedish) made from the sprouts. It is one of the most common banchan , as well as a basic ingredient of bibimbap .
