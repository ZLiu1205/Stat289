Nanakusa-no-sekku

The Festival of Seven Herbs or Nanakusa no sekku ( Japanese : 七草の節句 ) is the long-standing Japanese custom of eating seven-herb rice porridge ( nanakusa-gayu ) on January 7 ( Jinjitsu ).
