Fuqi feipian

Fuqi feipian ( Chinese : 夫妻肺片 ; pinyin : fūqī fèipiàn ; literally: "husband and wife lung pieces") is a popular Sichuan dish , served cold or at room temperature, which is made of thinly sliced beef and beef offal . Common ingredients in the modern version include beef heart, tongue and tripe, and a generous amount of various spices, including Sichuan pepper . True to its Sichuan roots, the desired taste should be both spicy and mouth-numbing. Despite its name, actual lung is rarely used. [1]
