Laziji

Laziji ( simplified Chinese : 辣子鸡 ; traditional Chinese : 辣子雞 ; pinyin : làzǐ jī ; literally: "spicy chicken"), is a dish of Sichuan cuisine . [1] It is a stir-fried dish, which consists of marinated then deep-fried pieces of chicken , dried Sichuan chilli peppers, spicy bean paste , Sichuan peppers , garlic, and ginger. [2]

Chicken with chilies (辣子鸡, pinyin: Là Zǐ Jī; literally "Spicy chicken") is a well-known Sichuan-style Chinese dish. It consists of marinated, deep-fried pieces of chicken that are then stir-fried with garlic , ginger , and chili peppers . [1] The chicken and chilies are served together and diners use chopsticks to pick out the pieces of chicken, leaving the chilies in the bowl. [2]
