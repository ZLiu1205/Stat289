"Hot and sour soup" ( simplified Chinese : ; traditional Chinese : ; pinyin : sun l tng ), a Chinese soup claimed variously by Mandarin and Sichuan cuisines as a regional dish. The Chinese hot and sour soup can be either vegetarian or meat-based, and often contains ingredients such as day lily buds, wood ear fungus, bamboo shoots , and tofu .
