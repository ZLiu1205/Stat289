Hayashi rice

Hayashi rice or Hashed beef rice (ハヤシライス) is a dish popular in Japan as a Western-style dish ( yōshoku ). It usually contains beef , onions , and button mushrooms , in a thick demi-glace sauce which often contains red wine and tomato sauce . This sauce is served atop or alongside steamed rice . The sauce is sometimes topped with a drizzle of fresh cream .
